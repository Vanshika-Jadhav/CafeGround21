About Us

100% Pure Veg. 100% Pure Joy.

The Ground 21 Story

Located in the heart of Chaitanya Park, Indrayani Nagar, Cafe Ground 21 was born out of a simple passion: serving exceptionally delicious fast food that happens to be completely vegetarian.

We pride ourselves on being a safe, clean, and vibrant space for the Bhosari and PCMC community. Whether you're catching up with friends over cold coffee, treating your family to a pizza night, or just grabbing a quick bite after work, we make sure every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and a lot of love.
